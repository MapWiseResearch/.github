# MapWiseResearch

**Your one-stop shop for everything elections.**

MapWiseResearch is an independent research organization behind **MapWisePolitics**, a platform focused on election forecasting, visualization, and political data analysis. Founded as a passion project in 2025, MapWisePolitics was created to make political forecasts and data-driven insights clear, accessible, and understandable to a broad audience.

---

## About MapWisePolitics

MapWisePolitics provides election forecasts, projects, and visualizations covering U.S. politics. Our goal is to combine rigorous data analysis with intuitive presentation, allowing users to engage with election modeling without needing a technical background.

Forecasts and data outputs are published transparently, with an emphasis on clarity, accountability, and openness.

## Transparency & Open Data

MapWiseResearch is committed to transparency and open access. Selected forecast outputs, datasets, and research artifacts are published publicly to support open analysis, reuse, and public understanding of election modeling.

Some repositories contain complex or non-standardized data intended for technically literate users. Each repository documents its scope and limitations.

## Repositories & Projects
